Octiv vs SpringCM

The Octiv approach will deliver more breadth of solution, tighter integration to provide a more connected process and broader analytics to measure client interaction across the entire sales and contract process.

Octiv and SpringCM offer two very different approaches to increasing productivity throughout the entire sales cycle. SpringCM is a cloud-based tool used primarily in the contract management aspect of the sales process. SpringCM allows users to store, organize, access and share content within a cloud-based document repository. SpringCM documents are delivered “offline” (word/PDF document).

On the other hand, Octiv is a sales productivity tool that dynamically assembles documents for each stage in the sales process: marketing collateral, presentations, proposals, quotes and contracts. Octiv delivers these documents “online-first” with the ability to deliver an offline document as well. The advantage of delivering via an HTML link is that content can be dynamic, i.e. video, diagram or variable content. In addition, engagement by a client can be tracked in much more detail than simply who opened a document or when it was opened. Octiv provides analytics around specific pages of content.

Why Choose Octiv Over SpringCM

  • Platform vs. Point Solution

    Octiv provides a holistic framework for the entire sales, sales operations, marketing and legal process, a strategy for an end-to end connected flow. While SpringCM, a point solution, is focused on contract management with some features that address other areas of the process.

  • Digital Documents and Analytics vs. Offline Documents with Limited Insight

    With Octiv you can deliver via an HTML link which provides much more detail around client interaction and engagement. Analytics can be shared across the entire sales, sales ops and contract processes. SpringCM focuses on delivering a Microsoft Word or PDF which provides limited, high-level analytics for one aspect of the process, contract management.

  • Connected Process vs. One Aspect of the Process

    Octiv offers an open API which allows for integration to any existing solution. The ability to easily integrate to any object in Salesforce to create either a standard or custom workflow.
