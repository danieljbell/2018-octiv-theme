Octiv vs Seismic

Seismic’s main focus is on content management, so it has less experience, less success, and has invested less in enterprise document generation than other vendors.

Octiv’s productivity platform makes it easy to create and deliver sales proposals, presentations and contracts with a single click of a button inside of Salesforce. This provides sales teams with an all-in-one platform for creating and delivering a personalized, online buying experience to every prospective customer — all while improving efficiency, sales productivity and gaining new buyer insights — a complete solution that other vendors like Seismic simply can’t replicate.

Seismic has become increasingly similar to Octiv over the years. When Seismic first launched, they focused on building a content management platform to allow salespeople to find and access sales materials for clients and prospects. More recently, Seismic has expanded their platform to include content and document generation functionality.

Octiv enables companies to use the information they have in CRM, CPQ, ERP and other systems to create, deliver and track materials online. Octiv offers insight, analytics and automation to sales and empowers hundreds of companies around the world to replace static, offline PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoints and paper-based sales materials with online sales materials. Compared to Seismic, Octiv enables sales teams to be more productive, effective and efficient in creating a customized and streamlined sales process for every customer.

Why Choose Octiv Over Seismic

  • Digital Delivery

    Static, offline PDF sales proposals and contracts no longer work in a world where everything and everyone are mobile and digital. Sales teams need a smarter way to sell and close deals faster with documents available anytime, anywhere and on any device.

  • Enterprise Document Generation

    Creating personalized documents for every prospect is a difficult experience that is manual, error-prone and fragmented. Octiv provides organizations with a better way to create accurate, brand-compliant and consistently up-to-date documents with real-time data and information.

  • Tracking and Analytics

    Unlike PDFs, Word docs and PowerPoint files, every Octiv-powered asset provides instant alerts that identify who opens, views, and forwards content to prioritize follow up, improve relevancy and increase sales.
