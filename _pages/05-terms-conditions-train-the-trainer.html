Train-the-Trainer Facilitation Overview

We share your most important goal: streamlining your complex sales process. That's why we offer a wide range of programmatic services to meet your company's complex needs and maximize your tech stack investment. This offering is ideal for clients looking for content for on-boarding business teams and providing best practices in content delivery to their designated training personnel.

This offering should not be considered as a replacement for Implementation Services.

Scope

  • Delivery of Train-the-Trainer content (4 hours)

Client Participation

Deliverables

  • Click-path document placed into the Client's Octiv account

  • Best practices document on Octiv training delivery
  • One hour walk through session to review the above deliverables
