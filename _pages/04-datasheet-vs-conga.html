Octiv vs Conga

As a point solution, Conga requires multiple integrations with other vendors in order to provide the same functionality of Octiv’s all-in-one platform.

Octiv and Conga take two different approaches to solving document generation challenges for organizations. Conga is considered to be a point solution that customizes documents or presentations by merging data directly from Salesforce into templates. Octiv is an all-in-one platform designed not only to auto-generate visually stunning online documents and presentations out of Salesforce, but also helps sales teams to organize, store and distribute selling materials. Octiv can also be used to capture a legally binding electronic signature, track when a document is opened and provides visibility into how long a recipient spent viewing specific pages of a document or slides of a presentation.

Octiv has had the opportunity to win several customers that were previously Conga customers. Customers that switched from Conga to Octiv were looking for simpler administration of complex configuration options along with faster back-end response times. The Octiv solution addresses the enterprise user that is looking for an all-encompassing tool that enables a user to create, track, negotiate and sign documents online.

Why Choose Octiv Over Conga

  • One Platform Solution

    Conga is a point solution and requires integrations with other vendors such as e-Signature, file repositories, CLM, CPQ, and SAM software to get the same functionality that Octiv offers out of the box. Octiv is an all-in-one platform.

  • Enterprise Document Generation

    First, Conga is a utility that merges data from Salesforce into documents. With Octiv, you can generate documents from not just Salesforce, but from multiple sources of data warehouses such as cloud storage, finance, CPQ and more. Secondly, once you generate a document with Conga it is out of date. With Octiv, documents are always up-to-date with real-time data and information from your CRM.

  • No Coding Required

    Set-up of the document templates can be a bit tedious in Conga. Advanced configuration is possible with Conga, but requires more advanced technical coding skills. With Octiv’s drag-and-drop user interface, no query writing is required. Octiv’s administrative functionality to manage the document templates is built for the regular business user.

  • Mobile-First Documents

    Unlike Conga, You can access 100% of the Octiv platform from a mobile device, allowing you to create, edit and view documents from anywhere on any device.

  • Increased Visibility

    Unlike Conga, Octiv comes with access to real-time reports and alerts. Octiv’s ability to track content usage, customer engagement and content effectiveness at the aggregate and user levels provides quick, easy access to essential information on your team’s success.
