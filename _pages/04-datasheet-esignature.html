Octiv eSignature

Accelerate the Electronic Signature Process

Signing a document is often a cumbersome process. Once an agreement has been drafted, multiple parties may need to sign it in a particular order. It can take days or even weeks to receive signatures from all parties when the document has to be printed, signed, scanned or faxed back. That time can translate to lost business or revenue.

Octiv’s electronic signature (eSignature) capabilities are just one element of a full document generation and document management platform. Using Octiv’s eSignature removes the friction of paper contracts and wet signatures, PDFs and faxed documents. With Octiv, users can complete documents in just minutes. Teams can generate, collaborate on, manage and execute transactional documents with Octiv’s eSignature. Octiv’s eSignature solution feature addresses a wide range of digital signing needs.

Key Capabilities
Legally Binding eSignature Octiv electronic signatures meet the United States Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce (ESIGN) Act and the Uniform Electronic Transactions Act (UETA). Octiv also complies with the UK’s Electronic Communications Act and Electronic Signatures Regulations, the Canadian Uniform Electronic Commerce Act and Australia’s Electronic Transactions Act.
Sign Online or In-Person Octiv provides the flexibility to sign electronically online or in-person, or download the document to sign and upload the signed documents into the system.
Document Templates Set up commonly used document templates just once with signature rules, dynamic text fields and conditional logic.
Signature Workflows Automate complex business workflows for one signer, multiple signers, countersigners or non-signers. 1
Document Formats Octiv supports multiple document formats such as native HTML5 documents, Microsoft Word and PDF. Download documents from your desktop or from file- sharing sites like Box, OneDrive and Google Drive, and upload them to Octiv.
Reporting View and customize reports to see which documents are out for signature, are awaiting signatures and more.
Audit Trail View a complete audit trail and history of signers. Octiv collects each party’s name, email address, IP address and a record of activity they took including time, date and document view length.
Notifications Receive alerts for actions like document views, signatures and more.
Internationalization The Octiv user interface is localized, supporting more than 16 languages and currencies.
Company Branding Customize documents and emails with company logo, fonts and colors.
API Generate and sign documents from within any application with Octiv’s open API.
Mobile Support Send, track, sign and manage documents anywhere, anytime, from any device.
