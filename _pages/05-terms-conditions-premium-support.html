Premium Support Terms & Conditions

Premium Support will be provided to Customer's Users for those Octiv Services in accordance with this Exhibit. Users can submit cases over the Web or by telephone. Octiv will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly respond to each case, and will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly resolve each case. Actual resolution time will depend on the nature of the case and the resolution. A resolution may consist of a fix, workaround or other solution in Octiv's reasonable determination.

System Administrators

"System Administrators" are Users Customer identifies as primary liaisons between Customer and Octiv for technical support. Customer shall identify between one and five Administrator Contacts. Customer shall notify Octiv whenever System Administrator responsibilities are transferred to another individual.

Customers' System Administrators shall be responsible for:

  • Submitting and/or approving, on behalf of Customer, any account level requested changes/updates; and
  • Developing and/or deploying troubleshooting processes with Customer's organization

Customer shall ensure System Administrators:

  • Are knowledgeable about the applicable Octiv Services in order to help resolve, and assist Octiv in analyzing and resolving, technical issues; and
  • Have a basic understanding of any problem that is the subject of a case, and the ability to reproduce the problem in order to assist Octiv in diagnosing such problem.

Reproducing Errors

Octiv must be able to reproduce errors in order to resolve them. Customer agrees to cooperate and work closely with Octiv to reproduce errors, including conducting diagnostic or troubleshooting activities as requested and appropriate. Also, subject to Customer's approval on a case-by-case basis, Users may be asked to provide remote access to their Octiv application and/or desktop system for troubleshooting purposes.

Severity/Urgency Levels

Reproducible errors that cannot promptly be resolved will be escalated to appropriate resources for further investigation and analysis. Issues are categorized and handled according to assigned severity / urgency levels, as follows:

  • Severity Level
    1. Critical
      Critical functionality/process issue, affecting all or most users, with no workaround available.
    2. High
      Persistent functionality/process issue, affecting one or more users, with no reasonable workaround available.
    3. Medium
      System performance issue or bug with a short-term workaround available.
    4. Normal
      Routine capabilities/informational inquiry; known system performance issue or bug with a reasonable workaround available.
  • Urgency Level
    1. Critical
      Issue has brought business to a halt and requires immediate resolution or workaround.
    2. High
      Issue is negatively impacting business but documents are generated with a short-term workaround.
    3. Normal
      Issue is a routine inquiry and non-business impacting.

Excluded Items

Octiv Premium Support does not include any of the following:

  • Assistance with passwords (Users should click the "Forgot your password?" link on the login page or contact their system administrator);
  • Assistance with User settings (Users should contact their system administrator);
  • Assistance with lockouts due to incorrect login (Users should contact their system administrator to unlock the account, or wait for the lockout period to expire);
  • Assistance in developing/building User-specific custom workflows;
  • Assistance with non-Octiv products, services or technologies, including implementation, administration or use of third-party enabling technologies such as databases, computer networks or communications systems; or
  • Assistance with the self-implementation of Octiv solution
