Typical Project Plan

Click each stage icon to see implementation stage.

  • Week 1: Initiate ~1 hour

    You'll receive immediate access to your account, Welcome Kit, and online resources. The Octiv Professional Services team will then conduct a kickoff call with you to understand your specific business needs, set goals for your project, and recommend next steps. You'll work on gathering initial content and connecting any integrations.

  • Week 2: Design ~6 hours

    You'll consult with the Octiv team to design your end-users' workflow, the dynamic logic of your documents, and the behavior of your integrations in detail. The Octiv team will provide customized documentation and diagrams of the proposed solution we're building.

  • Week 3-4: Build ~12 hours

    The Octiv team will configure account and integrations settings to your needs and build initial document templates according to our solution design.

  • Week 5-6: Test ~12 hours

    Octiv will test your solution internally and conduct a testing kickoff call with you to train your own testing participants. We'll address any issues found during testing and conduct a live web-based administrative training with your Octiv admin, so you can help make additional revisions or start building more use cases.

  • Week 7-8: Deploy ~3 hours

    We'll provide a customized user quick guide and best practices for training and deploying to your team. We'll also support you on migrating your solution to production.

  • Project Management ~6 hours

    Your Octiv Project Manager will coordinate regular communications and status updates, and manage project scope, tasks, and timeline, and provide general consulting as needed.
