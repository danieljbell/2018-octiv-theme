Technical Consultancy Overview

We share your most important goal: streamlining your complex sales process. That's why we offer a wide range of programmatic services to meet your company's complex needs and maximize your tech stack investment. Technical Consultancy provides technical guidance and best practice insights for optimal use and growth. Ideal for clients looking to improve and/or expand their use case for Octiv and who want recommendations and technical input into that effort.

This offering should not be considered as a replacement for Implementation Services.

Scope

  • Delivery of online / over the phone technical consultation session(s) (3 hours)

Client Participation

  • Availability of key stakeholders for participation in technical consultation session(s)
  • Pre-scheduling of technical consultation session(s)
  • Completion of "101" training content (Octiv general online application training)

Deliverables

  • Review existing primary unified document workflow
  • Identify areas of optimization and/or augmentation
  • 1-hour session to review the above deliverables
  • On-site option available - time & travel expenses apply
