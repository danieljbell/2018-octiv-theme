Content/Asset Management Overview

We share your most important goal: streamlining your complex sales process. That's why we offer a wide range of programmatic services to meet your company's complex needs and maximize your tech stack investment.

Uploading, maintaining and distributing fresh content is critical to a consistent brand promise. This offering is ideal for clients who prefer to spend their time in the creative process of developing content and leave the administration and maintenance of that content to the Octiv experts.

This offering must be purchased for a subscription term that is coterminous with Client's license subscription.

Scope

  • Annual account content management plan creation
  • Quarterly on-going asset review and conversion

Client Participation

  • Creation of all branded content
  • Availability for participation in content discovery discussions
  • Availability for review & signoff of asset library
  • Completion of "101" training content (Octiv general online application training)

Deliverables

  • Annual Content Management Plan
  • Quarterly Content Assessment (Utilization, Effectiveness, Engagement...etc.)
  • Quarterly review of offering utilization
