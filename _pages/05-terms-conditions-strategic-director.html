Strategic Director Overview

We share your most important goal: streamlining your complex sales process. That's why we offer a wide range of programmatic services to meet your company's complex needs and maximize your tech stack investment.

A Strategic Director provides enterprise-wide oversight for your Octiv platform use. Strategy, thought leadership and a program road map help you reach your overall goals and desired outcomes. This offering is ideal for clients with complex organizational structures who need a single point of contact to manage their strategic business relationship with Octiv.

This offering must be purchased for a subscription term that is coterminous with Client's license subscription.

Scope

  • Create an Annual Charter for the account's go forward plan
  • Work with customer to develop overall desired program desired outcomes, goals and objectives and to outline specific strategic projects in support of that plan
  • Produce and consult on unique reporting requirements
  • Consistently analyze and plan for ways to incorporate best practices
  • Help navigate the ever-changing world of sales & document productivity
  • Manage the overall activity of all Account programmatic offering purchases

Client Participation

  • Active participation in client strategy meetings
  • Availability for program scoping efforts

Deliverables

  • Account Charter w/ monthly updates
  • QBR Facilitation
  • Quarterly review of utilization across entire account structure
