Octiv Contract Management

Create and manage contracts more effectively

Contracts are mission-critical for B2B organizations. Managing the contracting process and keeping a library of up-to-date contract clauses is a daily struggle. Below are several key challenges many businesses face when dealing with contracts:

  • Lack of tight administrative controls over the contracting process can result in lost revenue
  • An inefficient and cumbersome negotiation process can cost hours of lost productivity.
  • Ensuring that contracts are accurate, reviewed appropriately, and signed by the authorized individuals is often a time-consuming and cumbersome process.
  • Contract drafts and the information necessary to create them are often scattered across departments and teams and disconnected from other key business applications and back office systems.

Octiv delivers a next generation contract lifecycle management (CLM) solution that makes it faster and easier to create, share, review, edit and sign contracts on any device. Octiv provides B2B organizations with the tools to facilitate the redline negotiation process and allow multiple parties to access the most current version of a contract anytime.

Unlike traditional contract lifecycle management providers, Octiv delivers contracts as online documents so they can be shared, edited, signed, and managed from any device without the need to download software or plugins. Octiv provides a level of collaboration and visibility into the status of a contract that no other solution on the market can provide.

Key Capabilities
Contract Drafting Automate contract creation with pre-approved templates and a centralized clause library
Contract Negotiation Negotiate and redline contracts online in real time. Octiv provides complete tracking of who made what changes in which versions.
Contract Approval Octiv integrates with the Salesforce Approval Workflow to route the draft of the right contract to the appropriate people for approval.
Contract Execution Sign agreements electronically with a legally binding electronic signature.
Contract Repository Archive and house all executed agreements in a secure and central location.
Contract Reporting Use our reporting capabilities to find all contracts with specific vendors and gain visibility into the status of a contract. Reports can be exported as a CSV format for analysis in other systems.
Contract Importing Easily migrate any number of contracts from your legacy system into the Octiv contract management repository. Octiv provides the ability to set metadata and tags upon import.
Centralized Clause Library Contract clauses and terms and conditions can be managed and controlled in a centralized library.
Microsoft Word Support Support for uploading track changes and comments from Microsoft Word.
Mobile Support Create, edit, negotiate, and sign contracts anywhere, anytime, from any device.
