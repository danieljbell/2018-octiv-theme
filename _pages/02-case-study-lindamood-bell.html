Before Octiv, Lindamood-Bell’s enrollment process was offline and took days or weeks to close. Today, the entire process is online and enrollment takes just minutes.

  • The Problem

    Lindamood-Bell’s paper-based enrollment process caused headaches for staff and students alike, and the company realized it needed a change. After migrating from a legacy system to Salesforce in late 2014, Lindamood-Bell was set with a single data source, but they needed a solution that could build complex documents with show/hide logic.

    Prior to Octiv, the company used a Word document template to fill in customer information, then printed and mailed the document to the customer. Customers signed a paper document and mailed it back to their chosen learning center location, making enrollment a days-long, offline process.

  • The Solution

    By creating an online version of the enrollment agreement with Octiv, Lindamood-Bell slashed days from the enrollment process and made it easy for customers to complete and sign the documents with DocuSign in just minutes. It’s also a significant time-saver for the sales team, who had to manually input customer data from offline documents into Salesforce.

The integration is completely seamless, because everything is in Salesforce, they never have to actually leave the CRM to create and send documents.

Elizabeth Hardman

Director of Business Solutions

Lindamood-Bell

  • The Results

    Lindamood-Bell’s recommendation document was even more complex than the enrollment agreement. With 68 possible versions, an online document would require hundreds of fields and show/hide logic – but Octiv consolidated all 68 versions into a single online template. This made the migration from the company’s legacy system to Salesforce complete: because the output was identical and generated from Salesforce, salespeople could more easily adopt the CRM more quickly and easily.

    With Octiv, Lindamood-Bell has transformed not only its sales process, but its customer experience. Now that the company’s salespeople and customers can create, review, sign and send documents completely online, Lindamood-Bell is looking forward to how Octiv can impact every stage of the sales process.
